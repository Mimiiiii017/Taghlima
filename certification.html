Official Maltese Language Certification

Congratulations on progressing through our lessons! To officially certify your language skills, we partner with licensed institutions that offer Maltese language exams. These certificates are recognised by employers, schools, and public institutions across Malta.

How It Works

  1. Complete all learning modules on this platform.
  2. Pass our internal practice assessments to qualify.
  3. Choose a partner institution from our list below.
  4. Register and sit for the official certification exam.
  5. Receive a printed and/or digital certificate upon passing.
