Essential medical vocabulary for healthcare professionals. Learn terms like "uġigħ" (pain), "mediċina" (medicine), "sptar" (hospital)...

Patient Communication

Learn how to communicate with patients in Maltese. Important phrases like "Fejn tuġgħak?" (Where does it hurt?), "Kemm ilek tħossok hekk?" (How long have you felt this way?)...

Emergency Situations

Critical vocabulary for emergency healthcare situations. Key phrases include "Sejjaħ l-ambulanza" (Call an ambulance), "Għajnuna!" (Help!)...

Restaurant Service
